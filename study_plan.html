How to Use the Resources

Daily Rhythm

  • Pre-rounds (20 min): Anki reviews only -- no new cards
  • Lunch (20 min): New cards (NSGY Boards) + 10 new Fluoro cards
  • Evening (25 min): Finish new card target for the day
  • After cases: Search Anki for the case topic tag -- drill 10 cards

Weekly Rhythm (Sunday)

  • 15 min -- Greenberg prep: Open AccessSurgery to next week's chapter. Skim headings, memorize 3 key numbers (doses, grades, thresholds)
  • Set Anki filter to next week's subdeck in NSGY Boards
  • 20 SANS questions: Any topic, timed, read all explanations carefully

Case-Linked

  • Night before cranial case: Open Rhoton atlas for that anatomy (5 min)
  • Night before spine case: Watch Operative Neurosurgery video for that approach
  • After tumor case: Pull WHO classification table + run Anki tumor tag
  • Week 16: Full timed SANS mock exam -- simulate real exam conditions

Anki Auto-Sync (no plugin required)

Run python3 ~/anki_sync.py in Terminal after each study session. It reads your Anki database directly -- Anki does not need to be open. It counts all cards reviewed today from NSGY Boards 2024 Updated, Dura Deck v2::TR Neurosurgery Board Review, and NSGY Fluoro, then opens this page with the count auto-applied. No manual entry needed.

Bay Area Events & Weekend Ideas

Matched to your off weekends and vacation blocks. Verify exact 2026-27 dates before booking. Ask Claude to refresh this list any time.

Vacation Block 1 — Aug 1-9
Nihonmachi Street Fair
Japantown SF — Aug 1-2
One of the largest Japanese-American street festivals on the West Coast. Taiko, food stalls, live J-pop, local vendors. Free to attend. Vacation starts right as it opens.
Japan Festival
Outside Lands
Golden Gate Park — ~Aug 7-9
SF's flagship music festival. Consistently strong headliners and the best food lineup of any US festival. Vacation ends right in time for the final weekend -- check 2026 lineup when announced.
Music Festival
Point Reyes National Seashore
Marin County — 1.5 hr drive
Lighthouse hike, tule elk reserve, and Hog Island Oyster Co for lunch. Best midweek to avoid crowds. One of the most scenic spots in Northern California.
Day Trip
Napa Valley Weekday
Napa/Yountville — 1.5 hr drive
Weekday Napa = no crowds. Oxbow Public Market for lunch, Castello di Amorosa for views, Yountville for dinner (Bouchon Bistro). Book Bouchon in advance -- it fills up.
Wine + Food
Santa Cruz
Santa Cruz — 1 hr drive
Classic California beach town. Boardwalk amusement park, West Cliff Drive coastal walk, Seabright Beach. Best on a sunny weekday. Add Capitola village on the way back.
Beach Trip
Oct 10-11 Free Weekend
Hardly Strictly Bluegrass
Golden Gate Park — early Oct (verify)
Completely FREE 3-day bluegrass and Americana festival. One of the best free music events in the US. Typically the first weekend of October -- confirm 2026 dates, it may fall Oct 2-4 or align with your weekend.
Free Festival
Fleet Week SF
SF Waterfront — mid-Oct (verify)
Blue Angels air show over the bay. Watch from Crissy Field, Fort Mason, or the Marina. Free from public areas. One of the most dramatic Fleet Week venues in the US.
SF Event
Castro Theatre
Castro District, SF
Classic SF movie palace with a Wurlitzer organ. Indie films, classic screenings, and themed movie nights year-round. Check calendar for October programming. Always something interesting.
Indie Film
Vacation Block 2 — Dec 5-13
SF Ballet: The Nutcracker
War Memorial Opera House, SF
SF Ballet's Nutcracker is one of the best productions in the country. Runs all of December. Upper balcony tickets under $50. Book in advance -- popular dates sell out fast.
Ballet
Great Dickens Christmas Fair
Cow Palace, Daly City — Nov-Dec weekends
Victorian London immersive fair with costumed actors, live entertainment, period food and gifts. Runs Sat-Sun in November and December. Fun, campy, and full of holiday energy.
Holiday Event
Lake Tahoe Skiing
Tahoe — 3.5 hr drive
December opens ski season at Heavenly, Northstar, and Palisades Tahoe. Book midweek to save on lift tickets and avoid crowds. Book lodging well in advance -- Tahoe fills up in December.
Ski Trip
Alamo Drafthouse SF
Mission District, SF
Reserved seating with a full dinner and cocktail service. Holiday film programming in December is always good. No talking policy strictly enforced -- the civilized way to watch a movie.
Film + Dinner
Vacation Block 3 — Feb 6-14
SF Chinese New Year Parade
Chinatown SF — ~Feb 13, 2027
Year of the Sheep 2027. The SF parade is the largest Chinese New Year parade outside Asia. Floats, lion dancers, firecrackers down Grant Ave. Typically the Saturday nearest the lunar new year -- falls right in your vacation.
Cultural Festival
Big Sur Winter Waterfalls
Big Sur — 2.5 hr drive
February is peak waterfall season. McWay Falls, Pfeiffer Beach purple sand, Bixby Bridge. Overnight at Fernwood Resort or Treebones (book months in advance -- sells out far ahead).
Overnight Trip
Monterey + Carmel
Monterey Peninsula — 2 hr drive
Monterey Bay Aquarium (world-class), 17-Mile Drive, Carmel-by-the-Sea village. February = gray whale migration season -- kayak tours available. Great day trip or overnight at an inn.
Day or Overnight
Valentine's at SFJAZZ
SFJAZZ Center, Hayes Valley SF
Valentine's week programming at SFJAZZ is reliably special. World-class performers in an intimate venue. Pair with dinner at Monsieur Benjamin or Rich Table nearby. Book tickets early -- sells out.
Jazz + Dinner
Vacation Block 4 — Apr 17-25
SF Cherry Blossom Festival
Japantown SF — mid-to-late April
Two weekends of sakura in Japantown. Grand Parade, taiko drumming, ikebana, Japanese food stalls. The second festival weekend almost always falls within this vacation block. Don't miss the taiko showcase.
Japan Festival
SF International Film Festival
Multiple venues, SF — late Apr/early May
SFIFF is the oldest film festival in the Americas. World premieres, retrospectives, and director Q&As. Passes or per-screening tickets. Check sffilm.org for 2027 lineup and dates.
Indie Film
Coachella Weekend 2
Indio CA — ~Apr 18-20 (verify)
Weekend 2 typically falls in this vacation window. 6-hr drive or short flight to Palm Springs. Book accommodations the moment the lineup drops in early January -- everything near Indio goes fast.
Music Festival
Yosemite Waterfalls
Yosemite — 3.5 hr drive
April is the single best month for Yosemite waterfalls -- all at peak flow after snowmelt. Yosemite Falls, Bridalveil, Vernal and Nevada Falls. Day-use permits required; book on recreation.gov weeks ahead.
National Park
VA Rotation Free Weekends — Year-Round Standbys
Punch Line SF / Cobb's Comedy
Financial District + North Beach, SF
Punch Line is one of the oldest comedy clubs in the US. Cobb's is larger with bigger touring headliners. Both run Fri-Sat shows. Tickets $20-35 online. Great for a low-key free weekend night.
Comedy
Japantown SF
Japan Center, Post St, SF
Kinokuniya bookstore, Marufuku ramen, Benkyodo mochi shop (a historic SF institution). The Japan Center malls have hidden import gems. SPiN sake bar nearby. Easy parking off Geary Blvd.
Japan + Food
Stern Grove Festival
Stern Grove, SF — Sundays, Jun-Aug
FREE outdoor Sunday concerts in a natural eucalyptus grove amphitheater. Eclectic lineup: classical, jazz, world music, dance. Bring a blanket and snacks. Very SF, very good.
Free Concert
Ferry Building Farmers Market
The Embarcadero, SF — Saturdays
Best farmers market in the Bay Area. Acme Bread, Humphry Slocombe ice cream, Blue Bottle coffee, Far West Fungi. Saturdays 8am-2pm. Free to browse. Walk the waterfront after.
Food + Market
Trick Dog + Comstock Saloon
Mission + North Beach, SF
Trick Dog (Mission): James Beard-nominated rotating themed menus, full food, no reservations. Comstock Saloon (North Beach): classic San Francisco cocktail bar, old-school atmosphere, excellent drinks.
Cocktail Bars
Half Moon Bay
San Mateo County — 30 min from Palo Alto
Closest beach town to Palo Alto. Sam's Chowder House on the pier, Mavericks Beach for big-wave views, Pigeon Point Lighthouse. Pumpkin patches in October. Perfect for a quick half-day reset.
